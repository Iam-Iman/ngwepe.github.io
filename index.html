About Me

Iman is a data scientist with a background in analytical chemistry who is inquisitive and self-driven. She is fluent in at least five official languages of her own country, South Africa, and is familiar with all eleven, solidifying her people skills. She enjoys translating raw data into knowledge that is simple to understand. She learned about data science when she was studying, made the decision to pursue it, and hasn't looked back since. Data cleaning, manipulation, modeling, and visualization are among her areas of expertise. Iman firmly believes in the value of teamwork, research, adjusting to the environment of the data sector, and having tenacity.
Gardening, hiking, monthly beach clean-ups, caring for her livestock, and crocheting are some of her favorite activities.

Heart Disease Prediction using Classification

In this study, I used a public dataset on heart illness. In Jupyter Notebook, I cleaned, wrangled, and explored the data. Developed numerous classification models and assessed their metrics before settling on Grid Search Logistic Regression as the final model. The model was then saved as a pickle file, which was then utilized in Spyder IDE to create an API using FastAPI and Heroku.
